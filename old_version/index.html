Hi, I'm Arlene

I'm a developer and designer.


Growing up in Los Angeles, California, I have always loved doodling on my notes and tackling math problems for hours at a time - traits that have paved my way toward computer science. For me, being a developer means solving problems, turning ideas into products, and creating tangible value through code.

I'm currently studying computer science at MIT. So far, I have worked as a software intern and researcher, tinkered with ideas at hackathons, and spent time on personal projects. Though I am foremost a developer, my passion lies in design. I see UI/UX design as a way to shape products based on the needs and preferences of its users. Through design and code, I hope to work on meaningful projects with great impact.
